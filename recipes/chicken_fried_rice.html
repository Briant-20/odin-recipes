Chicken fried rice

Standered chicken fried rice dish

One-skillet, ready in 20 minutes, and you’ll never want takeout again after tasting how good homemade is!! Way more flavor, not greasy, and loads of juicy chicken!!

Using cold rice for this dish works best, so cook it the night before, then leave it in the fridge overnight – 250g of dried rice will give you the cooked quantity you need.

Igredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Peas

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken
  2. Fry the rice
  3. Boil the peas
